Ravioli

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • All purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Olive Oil

For the filling:

  • Parmesan
  • Mozzarella
  • Provolone
  • Egg
  • Dried Parsley

For the sauce:

  • Your choice of ingredients

Steps

  1. Roll the dough and refrigerate for atleast 30 minutes
  2. Make the filling
  3. Make the sauce
  4. Assemble the ravioli
  5. Cook and bake the ravioli
  6. Serve
